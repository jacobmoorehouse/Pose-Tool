Welcome to our posing tool!

We will be building a posing tool that recreates the human body and allow artists to have a semi-realistic reference. Each part of the body will be able to rotate a full 360 degrees except exceptions that we feel will be needed. In order to do this we will program every limb as a straight, unbendable rod and each joint as a sphere. Then we will replace each rod with a 3D model of what object should be there. The user will be able to fully rotate the entire body by dragging the screen and perhaps even zooming in. Additionally, we will allow the user to save the current pose as a jpeg for future reference, and even change certain features of the human body, and changing its gender.

Drag to move the figure around. Use arrow keys to pan. To assist in selecting objects, zoom in more.


There are two types of controls. One type of controls keeps the object centered at (0,0,0) and the other type allows you to pan with the arrow keys.


Instructions

  • To select on a limb. When selected, the object should change to turquoise. If you are having trouble selecting, try zooming in by scrolling.

  • There are two lights available. To change intensiontiy change it's position in the z direction.

  • The menus are collapsible to minimize space.

  • Click the buttons on the bottom rotate the selected limb in the desired direction.

  • To change the view, drag to rotate the camera and zoom in by scrolling. Additionally, you can pan with the arrow keys.

  • If you have found that you have rotated the camera too much, then reset the camera by clicking on the respective button.
