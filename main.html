Welcome to our posing tool!

We will be building a posing tool that recreates the human body and allow artists to have a semi-realistic reference. Each part of the body will be able to rotate a full 360 degrees except exceptions that we feel will be needed. In order to do this we will program every limb as a straight, unbendable rod and each joint as a sphere. Then we will replace each rod with a 3D model of what object should be there. The user will be able to fully rotate the entire body by dragging the screen and perhaps even zooming in.

Codine is made of Junze He and Jacob Moorehouse.
